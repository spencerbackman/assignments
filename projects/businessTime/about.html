About

Hey there! My name is Talia and I am the owner of Mugshot Photobooth. I designed, built, and run Mugshot Phobooth primarily by myself but with the help of my husband and brother. I recently got married in June of 2017 and what inspired me to create my photobooth was that I couldn't find a reasonably priced photobooth in the Draper area that was actually cute! So off I went on my adventure of starting a business and helping other brides and event planners find what I was looking for myself. Because I have done lots of research on different Photobooths in Utah I know how hard it is to find someone who will help you get exactly what you're looking for. That is why I am completely open to your ideas and making your event exactly how you picture it! Don't hesitate to contact me or ask any questions. I look forward to working with you :)
